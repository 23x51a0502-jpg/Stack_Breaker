# Master Production Book

## Budget Planning
**Detailed Budget Plan for Sci-Fi Short Film: Moon Base Heist**

**Project Overview:**
The moon base heist is a sci-fi short film that tells the story of a group of skilled thieves who attempt to steal a valuable resource from a heavily guarded moon base. The film will feature a mix of action, suspense, and drama, with a focus on visual effects and production design.

**Estimated Totals:**

* **Total Budget:** $250,000
* **Pre-Production:** $50,000 (20% of total budget)
* **Production:** $120,000 (48% of total budget)
* **Post-Production:** $50,000 (20% of total budget)
* **Visual Effects:** $20,000 (8% of total budget)
* **Contingency Fund:** $10,000 (4% of total budget)

**Department Breakdown:**

1. **Pre-Production:**
	* **Script Development:** $10,000 (20% of pre-production budget)
	* **Location Scouting:** $5,000 (10% of pre-production budget)
	* **Casting:** $10,000 (20% of pre-production budget)
	* **Crew Assembly:** $10,000 (20% of pre-production budget)
	* **Equipment Rental:** $5,000 (10% of pre-production budget)
	* **Permits and Licensing:** $5,000 (10% of pre-production budget)
	* **Miscellaneous:** $5,000 (10% of pre-production budget)
2. **Production:**
	* **Talent Fees:** $30,000 (25% of production budget)
	* **Crew Fees:** $20,000 (17% of production budget)
	* **Equipment Rental:** $20,000 (17% of production budget)
	* **Location Fees:** $10,000 (8% of production budget)
	* **Special Effects:** $10,000 (8% of production budget)
	* **Wardrobe and Makeup:** $5,000 (4% of production budget)
	* **Craft Services:** $5,000 (4% of production budget)
	* **Miscellaneous:** $10,000 (8% of production budget)
3. **Post-Production:**
	* **Editing:** $15,000 (30% of post-production budget)
	* **Visual Effects:** $15,000 (30% of post-production budget)
	* **Sound Design:** $5,000 (10% of post-production budget)
	* **Music Licensing:** $5,000 (10% of post-production budget)
	* **Color Grading:** $5,000 (10% of post-production budget)
	* **Mixing and Mastering:** $5,000 (10% of post-production budget)
4. **Visual Effects:**
	* **CGI:** $10,000 (50% of visual effects budget)
	* **Compositing:** $5,000 (25% of visual effects budget)
	* **Animation:** $3,000 (15% of visual effects budget)
	* **Motion Graphics:** $2,000 (10% of visual effects budget)

**Contingency Plans:**

1. **Weather Contingency:** In case of bad weather, the production team will have a backup plan to shoot indoor scenes or use a tent to cover the outdoor set.
2. **Talent Contingency:** In case of talent unavailability, the production team will have a backup plan to cast a new actor or adjust the script to accommodate the change.
3. **Equipment Contingency:** In case of equipment failure, the production team will have a backup plan to rent alternative equipment or use a different vendor.
4. **Location Contingency:** In case of location unavailability, the production team will have a backup plan to use a different location or adjust the script to accommodate the change.

**Risk Management:**

1. **Insurance:** The production team will purchase insurance to cover equipment, talent, and location risks.
2. **Contingency Fund:** The production team will allocate 4% of the total budget to a contingency fund to cover unexpected expenses.
3. **Risk Assessment:** The production team will conduct a thorough risk assessment to identify potential risks and develop mitigation strategies.

**Conclusion:**
The moon base heist is a complex and ambitious project that requires careful planning and budgeting. By allocating a significant portion of the budget to pre-production, production, and post-production, the team can ensure that the film is well-planned, well-executed, and well-finished. The contingency plans and risk management strategies will help mitigate potential risks and ensure that the project stays on track and within budget.

## Casting Studio
**Casting Call: "Moonlit Heist" - A Sci-Fi Short Film**

**Project Description:**
We are seeking talented actors to join our cast for a thrilling sci-fi short film, "Moonlit Heist". The story follows a group of skilled thieves who attempt to pull off a daring heist on a secure moon base, but soon discover that they are not the only ones with secrets to hide. The film will be a tense, action-packed ride with a mix of clever twists and turns.

**Key Roles:**

1. **Alex "The Ghost" Chen** (Lead)
	* Personality: Charismatic, confident, and calculating. Alex is the mastermind behind the heist.
	* Physical Description: Asian male, 30s, athletic build, short hair, and piercing eyes.
	* Casting Aura: We are looking for an actor who can convey a sense of calm, collected intelligence, with a hint of mischief. Think "Ocean's Eleven" meets "Inception".
2. **Dr. Sophia Patel** (Supporting)
	* Personality: Intelligent, resourceful, and determined. Sophia is a brilliant scientist who becomes embroiled in the heist.
	* Physical Description: Indian female, 20s, long dark hair, and expressive brown eyes.
	* Casting Aura: We need an actress who can bring a sense of authenticity and vulnerability to the role, with a hint of quiet strength. Think "The Martian" meets "Ex Machina".
3. **Victor "The Bull" Martinez** (Supporting)
	* Personality: Tough, loyal, and hot-headed. Victor is the muscle behind the heist.
	* Physical Description: Latinx male, 30s, muscular build, shaved head, and a distinctive scar above his left eyebrow.
	* Casting Aura: We are looking for an actor who can bring a sense of intensity and physicality to the role, with a hint of humor and charm. Think "Die Hard" meets "The Expendables".
4. **Commander Sarah Taylor** (Supporting)
	* Personality: Authoritative, sharp-witted, and fiercely protective. Sarah is the commander of the moon base.
	* Physical Description: Caucasian female, 40s, short blonde hair, and a no-nonsense attitude.
	* Casting Aura: We need an actress who can convey a sense of command and authority, with a hint of warmth and empathy. Think "Star Trek" meets "The X-Files".

**Additional Roles:**

* **Moon Base Security Guards** (Background Actors)
* **Heist Team Members** (Supporting Actors)
* **Scientific Researchers** (Background Actors)

* Headshots and resumes should be submitted electronically to [casting@moonlitheist.com](mailto:casting@moonlitheist.com).

* Self-taped auditions are welcome, but not required at this stage.

**Audition Dates:**

* Initial auditions will be held on February 20th and 21st.
* Callbacks will be scheduled on a case-by-case basis.

**Production Dates:**

* Principal photography will take place from March 15th to April 10th.
* Location: A combination of studio and on-location shoots in the Los Angeles area.

**Compensation:**

* Competitive rates for SAG-AFTRA and non-union actors.

We look forward to reviewing your submissions and finding the perfect cast for "Moonlit Heist"!
